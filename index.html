Frequently Asked Questions

Is Fest free?

Yes, you can download Fest for free and start playing right away. Some game modes require a one-time purchase, and once you buy them, they’re yours forever. You also have the option to subscribe and unlock all content.

Do I need a subscription to play?

No. The subscription is optional. You can buy only the game modes you want and play them without limits. The subscription is for those who want full access to all game modes, but you can cancel anytime.

The subscription renews automatically, but you can cancel it anytime from your Apple or Google account. Don’t worry, you’ll still have access until the end of your paid period.

Are the game modes I buy mine forever?

Yes. When you purchase a game mode, it's yours for life. No hidden fees or recurring charges.

Does Fest get new content updates?

Yes. We’re constantly adding new cards and game modes to keep Fest fresh and fun.

Can I play Fest offline?

Yes. Fest doesn’t require an internet connection to play the game modes you’ve already purchased or if you have a subscription. However, you will need internet to download new updates.
