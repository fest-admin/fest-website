1. Introduction

Welcome to Fest! Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how Fest Global LLC ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects your personal data when you use our mobile application, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service"). By accessing or using Fest, you agree to this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not use our Service.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following types of information:

2.1. Information You Provide Directly

  • Account Information: Name, email address, date of birth, username, password, and other profile details.
  • Payment Information: When purchasing digital goods (such as virtual card decks) or subscriptions, we collect payment details (processed securely via third-party payment providers).
  • User-Generated Content: If you contribute content, such as custom cards or feedback.
  • Communications: If you contact us for support or inquiries.

2.2. Information We Collect Automatically

  • Device & Usage Data: IP address, device type, operating system, app usage statistics, and interaction logs.
  • Cookies & Tracking Technologies: We use cookies and similar technologies to enhance user experience and analytics.
  • Advertising Identifiers & Analytics SDKs: We use device identifiers such as the Android Advertising ID (AAID) or Apple Identifier for Advertisers (IDFA), as well as third-party analytics SDKs (including Meta SDK and others), to measure app installs, campaign effectiveness, and user engagement in compliance with applicable privacy laws.

2.3. Information from Third Parties

  • Social Media Login (if applicable): If you log in via Google, Apple, or other platforms, we receive limited data per their privacy policies.
  • Payment Providers: If you make purchases, we receive confirmation from our payment partners.

3. How We Use Your Information

We process personal data for the following purposes:

  • To provide and improve the Service.
  • To process transactions.
  • To personalize user experience.
  • To communicate updates, support, and marketing messages (with consent).
  • To ensure security and prevent fraudulent activity.
  • To comply with legal obligations.

3.1 Legal Basis for Data Protection

We process personal data under the following legal bases:

  • Contractual necessity: To provide and maintain the Service, process transactions, and deliver purchased content.
  • Legitimate interest: To analyze app performance, ensure security, and prevent fraud.
  • Consent: For analytics, cookies, and marketing attribution (e.g., Meta SDK, advertising identifiers).
  • Legal obligation: when required to comply with applicable laws or tax requirements.

4. Sharing & Disclosure of Data

We do not sell your personal data. However, we may share it in the following cases:

  • With Service Providers: Third-party vendors assisting in payment processing, hosting, analytics, and security.
  • For Legal Compliance: When required by law, governmental requests, or to protect our rights and users.
  • In Business Transactions: If Fest is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale, your data may be transferred under similar privacy protections.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your region, you may have the following rights:

  • Access & Correction: You can request a copy of your data and make corrections.
  • Deletion: You may request account deletion, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-Out of Marketing: You can unsubscribe from promotional emails at any time.
  • Restrict Processing: Under certain conditions, you can limit data processing.

To exercise these rights, contact us at hi@festapp.fun.
